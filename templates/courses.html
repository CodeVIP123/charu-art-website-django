Create rich textures and luminous colours with pure pigment, achieving painterly effects and brilliant intensity.

Unleash bold vibrancy and striking graphic impact with this opaque, matt medium ideal for dynamic illustration.

Capture human likeness and emotion by mastering anatomical proportions and nuanced expressive techniques.

Pencil Sketching

Develop foundational precision and observational skills for capturing form, perspective, and detail.
