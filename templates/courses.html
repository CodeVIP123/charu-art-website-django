We offer a variety of courses including:

Pencil Shading

In our professional art course, you will delve into the foundational art of Pencil Shading, exploring its capacity to render form, texture, and light through subtle tonal variations. You'll master a range of techniques, from delicate cross-hatching to smooth blending, empowering you to create incredibly realistic and expressive monochromatic drawings with precision and depth.

Charcoal

Our professional art course delves into the powerful medium of Charcoal, celebrated for its ability to create dramatic contrasts, rich tonal values, and raw expressive marks. You'll master techniques for building deep blacks, subtle greys, and luminous highlights, allowing you to produce evocative drawings with profound depth and striking visual impact.

Oil Pastel

Our professional art course guides you through the versatile world of Oil Pastels, a vibrant medium offering the best of both drawing and painting with their creamy texture and intense pigmentation. You'll master techniques for rich layering, impasto effects, and smooth blending, enabling you to create expressive works with dynamic colour and painterly depth.

Soft Pastel

Our professional art course delves into the expressive potential of Soft Pastels, a vibrant medium known for its pure pigment and remarkable ability to create luminous blends, rich textures, and spontaneous marks. You'll master techniques for layering, blending, and building depth, enabling you to achieve painterly effects and capture atmospheric qualities with unparalleled immediacy and brilliant colour intensity.

Poster Colours

Our professional art course introduces you to the dynamic world of Poster Colours, a medium prized for its opaque coverage and matt finish, allowing for striking, graphic illustrations and vibrant, flat colour applications. You'll master techniques to achieve strong visual impact and crisp lines, making them an excellent choice for developing bold compositions and exploring illustrative styles with immediate, impactful results.

Water Colours

Our professional art course invites you to discover the captivating world of Water Colours, a medium celebrated for its luminous transparency and delicate layering capabilities that evoke a unique sense of light and atmosphere. You will master essential techniques, from vibrant washes to precise detailing, allowing you to capture fleeting moments and express nuanced emotions with breathtaking fluidity and ethereal beauty.

Calligraphy

In our professional art course, you will embark on a journey to master Calligraphy, an exquisite art form that transforms script into visual poetry. Through dedicated practice, you'll learn the disciplined strokes, historical letterforms, and precise techniques required to create elegant and expressive lettering, imbuing your written work with timeless beauty and personal flair.

Mandala

Our professional art course offers a deep dive into the meditative practice of Mandala Art, exploring its rich history, intricate geometry, and profound symbolism as a tool for mindfulness and creative expression. You'll master the precise techniques of symmetrical design, from foundational grids to elaborate patterns, enabling you to craft captivating mandalas that resonate with balance and inner peace.

Acrylic Painting

In our professional art course, Acrylic Colours are not just paints; they're a dynamic foundation for unleashing your artistic vision. Their quick-drying nature and brilliant pigmentation offer unparalleled versatility, allowing you to master techniques from bold impasto to delicate washes. By deeply engaging with this adaptable medium, you'll build a robust understanding of color theory and application, equipping you with essential skills for a confident and expressive artistic practice.

Oil Painting

Our professional art course delves into the rich tradition of Oil Painting, a medium revered for its luminous depth, seamless blendability, and extended drying time that allows for thoughtful refinement. Through mastering this classic technique, you'll gain unparalleled control over color, light, and texture, empowering you to create works with enduring beauty and profound artistic expression.

Portrait Study

Our professional art course offers an in-depth Portrait Study, guiding you through the intricate nuances of capturing human likeness, expression, and individual character. You'll master essential anatomical proportions, lighting techniques, and the subtle art of conveying emotion, enabling you to create compelling and realistic portrayals with sensitivity and skill.

Pencil Sketching

In our professional art course, you'll hone your observational skills and precision through dedicated Pencil Sketching, a fundamental discipline for capturing form, perspective, and detail. You'll master a variety of techniques, from crisp outlines to nuanced tonal rendering, building the essential draughtsmanship needed to translate the world onto paper with accuracy and artistic flair.
