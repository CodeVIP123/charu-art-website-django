Welcome to Charu’s Canvas Art Classes, nurturing creativity since 2015. We proudly teach art to all age groups, from young beginners to passionate adults.

Our mission is not just to teach—but to empower. Many of our students, especially homemakers, have gone on to start their own art classes after learning with us.

We provide platforms to showcase talent through reputed art exhibitions, helping students build confidence and gain recognition.

With 1000+ students from 10+ countries across the globe, we offer both online and offline classes, making learning art accessible to all.

Along with regular courses, we also conduct various creative workshops throughout the year, ensuring continuous learning and fun.
